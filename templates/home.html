Did He Swing*?

When a batter swings at the first pitch of an at-bat and records an out, can we predict whether the next batter will swing at the first pitch? Based on inning number, quality of contact (estimated wOBA from launch angle and exit velocity), and outs recorded, I hoped to be able to predict this with high accuracy. The implications of making accurate predictions are huge for pitchers. With a difference of ~0.07 runs between an 1-0 count and an 0-1 count, stealing strikes in this quite common situation is incredibly valuable.
